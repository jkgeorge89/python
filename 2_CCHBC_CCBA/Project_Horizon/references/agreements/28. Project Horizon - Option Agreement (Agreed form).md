# GRANT OF CALL OPTION

Subject to Clause 4.1, Titan hereby grants to Olympus BV an irrevocable right during the Call Option Period to purchase the Option Shares held by it for the Purchase Price (the **"Call Option"**).

The Call Option may be exercised by Olympus BV:

if the Option Antitrust Approval was not granted for acquisition of the entire share capital of the Company prior to the date of this Agreement, at any time during the Call Option Period (subject to Clause 4.1) in respect of all (and not part) of the Option Shares. Notwithstanding the above, if upon the first exercise of the Call Option (or prior to that), Olympus BV obtains the relevant anti-trust approvals for acquisition of all (and not part) Option Shares, then the Call Option may be exercised in respect of all or part of the Option Shares at Olympus BV's sole discretion including by way of multiple exercises over time by issuance of a Call Option Exercise Notice to Titan in writing (with a copy to the Titan Guarantor), provided that each Call Option Exercise cannot be:

1. for fewer than such number of Option Shares which constitute five per cent. (5%) of the aggregate issued share capital in the Company; or
2. in respect of such number of Option Shares which would result in Titan holding less than seven per cent. (7%) of the aggregate issued share capital in the Company, unless such Call Option Exercise would result in Titan no longer holding any shares in the Company thereafter.

if the Option Antitrust Approval was granted for acquisition of the entire share capital of the Company prior to the date of this Agreement, at any time during the Call Option Period (subject to Clause 4.1) in respect of all or part of the Option Shares at Olympus BV's sole discretion including by way of multiple exercises over time by issuance of a Call Option Exercise Notice to Titan in writing (with a copy to the Titan Guarantor), provided that each Call Option Exercise cannot be:

#### for fewer than such number of Option Shares which constitute five per cent. (5%) of the aggregate issued share capital in the Company; or

#### in respect of such number of Option Shares which would result in Titan holding less than seven per cent. (7%) of the aggregate issued share capital in the Company, unless such Call Option Exercise would result in Titan no longer holding any shares in the Company thereafter.

Olympus BV may, at its sole discretion, by written notice to Titan prior to the Option Completion Date, acquire all or part of the Option Shares pursuant to the Call Option, in whole or in part, through a nominee (which shall be a member of the Olympus Group), subject to reasonable customary "know your customer" diligence in respect of such nominee.

During the Call Option Suspension Period, the Call Option shall be suspended and the Call Option shall automatically revive upon the expiry of the Call Option Suspension Period. It is clarified that any Call Option Suspension Period shall not operate to extend the Call Option for a period beyond the fifth (5th) anniversary of the SPA Completion Date.

# GRANT OF Put OPTION

Olympus BV hereby grants to Titan an irrevocable right during the Put Option Period to sell the Option Shares held by it for the Purchase Price (the **"Put Option"**).

The Put Option may be exercised by Titan at any time during the Put Option Period in respect of all (but not part) of the Option Shares held by Titan at the relevant time, at Titan’s sole discretion (a **"Put Option Exercise"**) by issuance of a Put Option Exercise Notice to Olympus BV in writing (with a copy to the Titan Guarantor).

Without prejudice to the obligation to purchase all Option Shares pursuant to the Put Option, Olympus BV may, at its sole discretion, by written notice to Titan prior to the Option Completion Date, acquire all or part of the Option Shares pursuant to the Put Option through a nominee (which shall be a member of the Olympus Group), subject to reasonable customary "know your customer" diligence in respect of such nominee.

During the Put Option Suspension Period, the Put Option shall be suspended and the Put Option shall automatically revive upon the expiry of the Put Option Suspension Period. It is clarified that any Put Option Suspension Period shall not operate to extend the Put Option for a period beyond the sixth (6th) anniversary of the SPA Completion Date.
